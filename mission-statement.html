Mission Statement

At Shogy Nosara, our mission is to craft exceptional chocolate using only the highest quality, locally sourced, ingredients. Rooted in the beach community of Nosara and inspired by our Switzerland meets Californian spirit, we are a family-owned and operated business dedicated to sustainability and care for our planet. Every jar we fill is a reflection of our love for the earth, and our commitment to family values.

About Us

Rooted in Nosara. Crafted with Love.
We’re a family-owned chocolate company based in the heart of Nosara, Costa Rica, a wild and wondrous place where our passion for quality and sustainability first took root.
Our story began with a spark of inspiration, one of our founders, captivated by the idea of making chocolate in Costa Rica, brought home the tools of the trade from her native Switzerland. What started as a dream, blossomed into a full-bodied craft business dedicated to creating world-class chocolate spread, lovingly made in small batches.
At the heart of everything we do is a reverence for nature and eco friendly practices. We source only the finest ingredients, working hand-in-hand with local farmers who share our commitment to quality and care. Our production methods are kind to the earth, we have a workflow that has been thoughtout to be as green as possible. 
Being a family business means more than just working together, it means weaving love into every step. Our children are our first taste testers, and our relationships are built on trust. Every jar we craft is a reflection of our values and our belief that chocolate, can be a powerful force for good.
We’re grateful you found us, and we can’t wait to share our chocolate with you.
From our family to yours,
– The Shogy Team

About Us

Rooted in Nosara. Crafted with Love.

We’re a family-owned chocolate company based in the heart of Nosara, Costa Rica, a wild and wondrous place. Our story began with a spark of inspiration, one of our founders, captivated by the idea of making chocolate in Costa Rica, brought home the tools of the trade from her native Switzerland. What started as a dream, blossomed into a full-bodied craft business dedicated to creating world-class chocolate spread, lovingly made in small batches.

Being a family business means more than just working together, it means weaving love into every step. Our children are our first taste testers. We craft every single jar by hand.

We’re grateful you found us, and we can’t wait to share our chocolate with you.

From our family to yours,
– The Shogy Team

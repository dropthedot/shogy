Discover Our Signature Spread

Handcrafted in Nosara, our Chocolate Hazelnut Spread is made in small batches, it’s a silky, plant-based indulgence that celebrates nature, family, and craft.

The heart of our recipe stems from cacao nibs and cacao butter for a bold chocolate flavor and a luxurious, melt-in-your-mouth finish.

Our spread is infused with a creamy blend of roasted hazelnuts and cashews, creating a smooth texture and naturally nutty depth.

A touch of sea salt enhances the spread’s complexity, balancing sweet and savory with elegance.


This is chocolate with purpose.
